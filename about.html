About Michael Lee

Welcome to mikelee.co, a website of data visualizations, analysis, and inquiry. I'm a data analyst/programmer interested in sports, education, and psychology. My recent experience involves analyses relating to education measures (student demographics, attendance, performance) and sports research (player comparisons, arbitration information, and salary compensation). I keep this site to display my work and I hope you find it interesting! If you do, let me know!
